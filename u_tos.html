Just Press Play Use and Privacy Policies

Use of Just Press Play is governed by RIT’s existing Code of Conduct for Computer and Network Use and Privacy Policy.

In addition, we expect you not to cheat. That means don’t get someone else to earn an achievement for you, and don’t submit someone else’s work as your own.

If we believe you’ve violated any policies, we’ll contact you. Depending on the severity of the problem, we may also decide to temporarily suspend your account.

User-Submitted Content Guidelines

Any content you submit to Just Press Play (photos, text, or URLs) must meet the following requirements:

  1. is not in violation of any applicable laws, contractual restrictions, or other third party rights;
  2. does not contain false information and is not confidential;
  3. is wholly owned by you (or you have permission from the owner to use it);
  4. does not contain any malware, adware, viruses, spyware, bugs, worms or other malicious code; and
  5. does not contain any personal or private information.

If we believe that any content submitted by you is in violation of these requirements (or the RIT computer use or privacy policies), we will delete the content in question and notify you.

In addition to meeting the overall content guidelines, photos or other images that you upload should meet the following guidelines:

  1. JPG, GIF or PNG format.
