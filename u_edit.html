Privacy Settings

These settings define who can see your game activity. All logged-in users can see your display name, your real name, your profile, and your six-word bio. By default, only players you've accepted as friends can see your full profile, including what achievements and quests you've completed. You can choose to make your full profile visible to all players, or to make it visible to people outside the game. If you make your profile public, you'll also be able to share individual achievements you complete on sites like Facebook and Twitter.

Communication Settings

We send out an email newsletter every 2-3 weeks with information about new achievements and events. You can opt out below, but you'll make us sad!
